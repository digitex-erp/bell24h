About WebSockets in Bell24h

WebSockets provide a persistent, bidirectional connection between the client and server, enabling real-time data transfer with minimal overhead.

In the Bell24h platform, WebSockets are used for:

  • Real-time chat between buyers and suppliers
  • Instant RFQ notifications
  • Live bid updates
  • Real-time payment confirmations
  • Delivery status tracking

Our implementation includes automatic reconnection, message queuing during disconnections, and sound notifications for important events.
