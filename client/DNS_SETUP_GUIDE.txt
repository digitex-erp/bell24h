PROBLEM: DNS is managed in cPanel, not Namecheap
SOLUTION: Transfer DNS back to Namecheap BasicDNS

STEP 1: Transfer DNS to Namecheap
1. In Namecheap Advanced DNS page
2. Look for "Change DNS Type" link (red text)
3. Click "Change DNS Type"
4. Select "Namecheap BasicDNS"
5. Click "Save Changes"

STEP 2: Add CNAME Record
1. After DNS transfer, look for "ADD NEW RECORD" button
2. Click it
3. Select "CNAME Record" from dropdown
4. Enter:
   - Host: www
   - Value: c2d4dfd06805fbcd.vercel-dns-017.com
   - TTL: Automatic
5. Click "Save"

STEP 3: Wait for propagation (5-30 minutes)

STEP 4: Test your domain www.bell24h.com

ALTERNATIVE: If you have cPanel access, add the CNAME record there instead.
