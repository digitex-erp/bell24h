Frequently Asked Questions

How does the wallet system work?

Every user gets a RazorpayX wallet linked to their bank account. You can deposit funds, make payments, and receive payments through this wallet. We charge a small fee of ₹50/month for wallet withdrawals to encourage platform retention.

What is the escrow protection?

For high-value deals (above ₹5 lakhs), we offer an escrow service where funds are held securely until predefined milestones are met. We charge a 1-2% fee for this service, providing security for both buyers and suppliers.

How does invoice discounting work?

Through our integration with M1 Exchange, suppliers can get immediate liquidity by discounting their invoices. We charge a small 0.5% fee for this service, allowing suppliers to access their cash flow when they need it most.

What are SHAP explanations?

SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) is a technique we use to explain our AI matching decisions. It provides transparency by showing which factors contributed most to a particular supplier match, helping you understand why a supplier was recommended.

Is there a limit to how many RFQs I can create?

Free users can create up to 5 RFQs per month, while Pro and Enterprise users have unlimited RFQs. Our special Lifetime Free tier for early adopters allows 10 RFQs per month with no expiration.

How do you verify suppliers?

We verify suppliers through GST validation, third-party business checks, and performance history on our platform. Verified suppliers are marked with a badge, giving buyers additional confidence in their selection.
